RadioHead 

Stats - 1.5 damage, 0.8x damage multiplier, 2.3 fire rate, 0.8x fire rate multiplier, 1 speed, 8 range, 1.1x range multiplier, 2 shot speed, 0 luck

Starts with 1 red heart and 2 charge hearts, does not cry instead releases sound waves that has initially has a 45° arc, which gets weaker and wider as it travels, since it's sound it has piercing and spectral, it cannot use regular trinkets, instead it starts with an active item called recorder which automatically absorbs a trinket and fills 1 charge, the more charges it has the better the disc it creates, golden trinkets gives 2 charges, micro batteries doesnt give anything and mini batteries or charge hearts give 1 charge, maximum of 24 charges

Can be used with at least 6 charges, the more charges it has the more likely he will get higher quality discs

Car battery makes it have chance to increase disc quality by 1 after using it

Charge hearts - its a permanent heart storage that contains 1 heart, if the container is emptied, make the active item usable (or fills 2 charges based of the active item) and becomes a drained heart that can only store half a heart, if isaac has a drained heart container, mini batteries will recharge the heart instead of going to the active item

Birthright effect: recorder gains more charges from trinkets, using it has a 10(+luck)% chance to create an additional disc
